Description.

Established in March 2017 by "Friends of We Chinese in America", which is an American, non-profit organization recognized by the federal government.

Dr. Yang Sheng, a professional well known conductor and musician, act as the Arts director and permanent conductor.

Ma Ping, the publisher of We Chinese in America publications, is the president of the symphony.

The AmeriCal Youth Symphony (AYS) is a remarkable non-profit music organization located in the vibrant city of San Diego. Committed to using the power of music to serve the community, AYS is dedicated to nurturing the musical talent and leadership skills of young individuals. With a strong belief in the transformative power of music, AYS conducts numerous public performances that not only showcase the remarkable talents of its members but also serve as a means to give back to the community.

Through these inspiring performances, AYS aims to create unforgettable experiences for audiences while also raising awareness about important social issues. The organization actively calls for fundraising efforts to support and empower underserved communities, working towards making a positive impact on the lives of those in need. By harnessing the energy and passion of its young members, the American Youth Symphony aims to inspire, uplift, and create a harmonious future through the universal language of music.
