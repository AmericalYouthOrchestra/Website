Description.

Established in March 2017 by "Friends of We Chinese in America", which is an American, non-profit organization recognized by the federal government.

Dr. Yang Sheng, a professional well known conductor and musician, act as the Arts director and permanent conductor.

Ma Ping, the publisher of We Chinese in America publications, is the president of the symphony.

The symphony provides youth with a platform to showcase their musical talents and cultural exchanges between Chinese and the West. At present, there are more than 50 student musicians.
